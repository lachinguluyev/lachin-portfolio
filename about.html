Hi, I'm Lachin. I stepped into the world of 3D back in 2016, and somewhere along the way, it stopped being just a skill and became something I genuinely love doing every day. My background is in Engineering and Business, but creativity is what really pulled me in — and kept me here.

Since then, I've been working with design studios and architectural firms, turning ideas into visuals that don't just look good, but feel real and alive.

My main toolkit includes 3ds Max, Corona Renderer, Photoshop, Unreal Engine, AutoCAD, and ComfyUI. Lately, I've been pushing more into real-time experiences with Unreal Engine — building interactive applications where you can actually walk through and explore spaces. I also create animations and 360° tours to make projects more immersive and engaging.

I'm especially into anything that blends architecture, visualization, and interactivity. I like experimenting, trying new approaches, and working in creative spaces where ideas can evolve into something unexpected.

A few highlights along the way: I took 1st place in the 3D Visualization category at the Azerbaijan Design Awards 2025, and was featured as 3D Artist of the Month by the Renderize community in 2020. (There's more in the Awards section if you're curious.)

For me, design is more than visuals — it's about mood, atmosphere, and storytelling.

I work with clients from all over the world — remotely, hybrid, or on-site — always focused on creating something unique, thoughtful, and a bit beyond the ordinary.
