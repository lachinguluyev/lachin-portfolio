About

Lachin Guluyev

I'm Lachin Guluyev, a 3D Architectural Visualization Artist with nearly a decade of professional experience in creating high-end visual content for architecture and interior design.

My work is centered on transforming concepts into visually striking and realistic environments that communicate both function and emotion. I specialize in 3ds Max, Corona Renderer, and Photoshop, while also integrating Unreal Engine for real-time rendering and interactive experiences.

In parallel, I actively explore and apply AI-driven tools to enhance workflow efficiency, creativity, and visual quality — staying aligned with the future of digital design.

My professional journey has been recognized with multiple achievements, including 1st place in the Visualization category at the Azerbaijan Design Award. I have also been invited as an expert to various TV programs and design publications, and have received awards in several industry competitions.

I believe great visualization goes beyond technical execution — it's about atmosphere, storytelling, and precision. Each project I create aims to deliver a refined, immersive, and timeless visual experience.
